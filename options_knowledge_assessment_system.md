# Options Knowledge Assessment System for Wealthsimple

## Current Wealthsimple Investor Assessment Framework

### Regulatory Requirements (Canada)
**IIROC "Know Your Client" (KYC) Requirements:**
- Investment knowledge and experience
- Risk tolerance assessment
- Investment objectives and timeline
- Financial circumstances
- Suitability determination

**CSA National Instrument 31-103:**
- Ongoing suitability assessments
- Product-specific knowledge requirements
- Documentation of investor understanding
- Regular review and updates

### Wealthsimple's Current Approach (Likely Structure)

**1. Initial Risk Assessment:**
```
Questions like:
- "How would you react if your portfolio dropped 20% in a month?"
- "What's your investment timeline?"
- "How familiar are you with different investment types?"
- "What's your primary investment goal?"
```

**2. Experience-Based Questions:**
```
- "Have you invested in stocks/ETFs/mutual funds before?"
- "How long have you been investing?"
- "What's the largest loss you've experienced?"
- "Do you have experience with leveraged products?"
```

**3. Knowledge Testing:**
```
- "What happens to bond prices when interest rates rise?"
- "Which is riskier: individual stocks or diversified ETFs?"
- "What does market volatility mean to you?"
```
